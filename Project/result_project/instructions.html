Instructions & Examination Rules

  • Carry your admit card and a valid ID proof on the examination day.
  • Electronic devices such as mobile phones, smart watches are not allowed.
  • Students must reach the exam centre at least 30 minutes before reporting time.
  • No re-entry is allowed once the exam begins.
  • Rough sheets will be provided in the exam hall.
  • Follow all instructions given by the invigilator.
