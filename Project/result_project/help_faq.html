Help / Frequently Asked Questions

1. What if I forget my roll number?

You should contact the exam authority with your registered email or phone number.

2. What if the result page is not loading?

Check your internet connection or try again after some time.

3. Can I request revaluation?

Revaluation procedure will be announced after result declaration.

4. Where can I get official notices?

All notices will be posted on the examination authority's official website.
